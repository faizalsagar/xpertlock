WHAT MAKES A STRONG PASSWORD:

  • It includes a mix of special characters, numbers, and symbols to have a combination that is not easy to guess.
  • It has at least 12 characters (but preferably even longer) and includes letters and numbers, in addition to the special characters and symbols. We recommend 64 characters as it allows the use of passphrases - a sequence of words.
  • It is unique and not re-used across multiple online accounts.
  • It is regularly updated - make sure to refresh your passwords at least once every few months.
  • It is not stored physically - nothing is more vulnerable than a Post-It note on your monitor.

WEAK PASSWORD EXAMPLES:

  • 1234567
    • Consecutive numbers aren't unique to you and don't use a variety of characters and symbols.

  • JaneDoe (Your name)
    • This information about you is easily available and doesn't use a variety of characters and symbols.

  • JaneDoeJr (Your child's name)
    • This information about you is easily available and doesn't use a variety of characters and symbols.
